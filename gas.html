WHY CHOOSE KEN HALL GASFITTERS?

Ken Hall Gasfitters are fully licensed, insured, and highly experienced in both domestic and commercial gas fitting.

For over 40 years, we have provided ongoing commitment to our customers with exemplary after-sales service and quality workmanship. We partner with reputable suppliers and offer warrantees on all gas installations and repairs.

From gas leak detections to full appliance installations, your safety is our top priority. We guarantee safe, efficient work that complies with all Australian regulations.
