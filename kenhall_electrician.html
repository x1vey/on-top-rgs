About us

Ken Hall Electricians operate with the same family values and commitment to service that you have come to expect from Ken Hall and our team of trusted and experienced tradespeople.

Locally owned and operated in Adelaide, we ensure total peace of mind with up-to-date knowledge on Australian Standards, Fire Safety and Workplace Safety. You can be confident a skilled Ken Hall Electrician will perform all work safely in your home, discuss your concerns and solve your electrical problem efficiently.

Lighting, power points, fans and appliance installation through to full house re-wiring, no electrical job is too big or small.

What areas do Ken Hall Electricians service?

Our team provides electrical services to all areas of Greater Metropolitan Adelaide. You’ll see our vans providing services from areas in the north such as Gawler, Munno Para and Salisbury, to suburbs in the south such as Aldinga, Goolwa and Victor Harbor. From east to west, we also service areas surrounding Mount Barker, Birdwood and Golden Grove to Port Adelaide and Brighton…Plus everywhere in between!
