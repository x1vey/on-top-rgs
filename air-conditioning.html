NEED AN AIR CONDITIONER SERVICE?

Here are some answers to common questions to keep your system running smoothly.

What should I do if my air conditioning unit is not heating or cooling?
Check your thermostat settings and ensure filters are clean. If the issue persists, our expert technicians can diagnose and repair it promptly to restore your comfort.
What to check if my air conditioning unit is not blowing air?
Ensure that your unit is receiving power and check your circuit breaker. A faulty fan or motor may require professional repair from our qualified tradespeople.
What does it mean if I'm getting error codes on my air conditioning unit?
Error codes indicate specific technical faults. It's best to turn off the system and contact Ken Hall Plumbers to accurately interpret the code and fix the underlying issue securely.
What are the benefits of upgrading to the latest air conditioning technology?
Upgrading provides significantly improved energy efficiency, better climate control, quieter operation, and advanced smart-home integrations, saving you money on power bills in the long run.
